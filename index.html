HOW THE TUBES WORK

It's as easy as crawl, settle, study, done.

1
🎒

Drop Off

Parents drop kids at the Tube Hatch between 3–5 PM. We scan their backpack and load their assignments into The System™.

2
🔵

Enter the Tubes

Kids pick a color-coded tube based on their subject. Red for math, blue for reading, green for science. They crawl to their study pod.

3
📚

Pod Study Time

Each tube leads to a cozy, lit study pod with a desk, supplies, and a Tube Tutor™ available via intercom for help.

4
🎉

Tube Slide Out

When homework is done and verified, kids earn Tube Tokens™ and exit via the celebratory Victory Slide into the pickup zone.

EXPLORE THE TUBE ZONES

Every tube leads somewhere awesome. Each zone is designed for maximum focus and minimum boredom.

🔢

The Math Maze

A twisting red tube network where every junction has a math problem. Solve it to unlock the next section. Pods have whiteboards on every surface.

Grades K-8
📖

The Reading Reef

Soft blue tubes lead to underwater-themed pods with bean bags, reading lights shaped like anglerfish, and total silence enforced by the Reef Warden.

All Ages
🔬

The Science Spirals

Transparent green tubes so kids can see the others studying around them. Pods include mini lab stations with safe experiment kits.

Grades 3-8
✏️

The Writing Hive

Hexagonal yellow pods connected by honeycomb tubes. Gentle buzzing ambient sound proven to boost creative writing output by 340%.*

*Not proven
🌐

The History Tunnels

Tubes decorated to look like time periods. Crawl through ancient Egypt to reach your pod. Each pod has historical artifacts (replicas) on display.

Grades 2-8
🏆

The Victory Tube

Not a study zone — this is the reward! A giant spiral slide tube with light effects and confetti that shoots kids into the pickup lobby when they're done.

TUBEY THE WORM

a.k.a. "The Green Machine" · Est. 2019

Tubey is a 6-foot-tall bookworm who lives inside the tubes and pops out to high-five kids who finish their homework. He smells like fresh erasers and optimism. He can't actually help with math but he tries really hard.

Tubey appears at every grand opening, birthday party, and whenever a kid finishes all their homework for the week (Tubey Tuesdays™). He has his own line of plush toys available at the Tube Shop.
